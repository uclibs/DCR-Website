University of Cincinnati Digital Press

The following online exhibits were developed by the University of Cincinnati Digital Press in collaboration with institutions in the Cincinnati area. They represent part of the effort which resulted in the monograph publication Art as Image: Prints and Promotion in Cincinnati, Ohio. The print publication has a supplement which appears as a separate online publication.
